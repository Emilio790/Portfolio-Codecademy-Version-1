About Me

Getting to know as much as I can, about a country's gastronomy, architecture and any other type of cultural practices have always been on top of my list when traveling around the world. Maybe that's why I'm so in love with my country. It's just amazing!

I traveled around the world for a couple of years, working and studying Business Administration at the same time. Something didn't felt right about the career I chose and decided to find something else.

I gave programming an opportunity after reading and watching videos about it being a great option for studying. I found out about codecademy and started one of the paid certified courses. I felt in love with Frontend development.
